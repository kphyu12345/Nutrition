Omelette Au fromage

Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 400 calories
  • 27g Protein

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a small mixing bowl. Season with a pinch of salt, then whisk vigorously with a fork or whisk until the yolks and whites are fully combined and the mixture is smooth, slightly frothy, and uniform in color. This helps create a tender and fluffy texture.
  2. Once the eggs are mostly set but still slightly soft and glossy on top (about 90% cooked), sprinkle your cheese evenly over one half of the omelette. Carefully fold the other half over the cheese using your spatula to create a half-moon shape. Let it cook for another 20–30 seconds to allow the cheese to melt inside.
  3. Once the eggs are cooked about 90 percent, add cheese to one half and fold the omelette onto itself. Optionally, if you’d like to toast the omelette add additional butter to the pan and increase the heat slightly, and gently tilt the pan in the direction of the omelette to toast the bottom and get more color.
  4. Carefully add your omelette to your plate. Serve and enjoy.
