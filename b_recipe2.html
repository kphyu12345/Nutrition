Egg White and Spinach Scramble

Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 120 calories
  • ≈18g protein
  • 2g fiber

Instructions

  1. Rinse the morning glory thoroughly to remove any dirt. Shake off excess water and chop into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolks, placing the whites in a small bowl.
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and add a teaspoon of olive oil or use a light spray to coat the surface.
  4. Add the chopped morning glory and stir-fry quickly for about 1-2 minutes.
  5. Pour the egg whites evenly over the morning glory. Let them cook undisturbed for 30-60 seconds to allow the base to set slightly.
  6. Using a silicone spatula, gently stir and fold the mixture,
  7. Lighty season with salt and pepper.
