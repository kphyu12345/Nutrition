Mango Chia Smoothie

Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 412 calories
  • 9.3g Fiber
  • 30g Protein

Instructions

  1. If you’re not using pre-cut frozen mango, peel and chop a ripe mango into cubes and freeze for at least 2–3 hours before blending.
  2. In a high-speed blender, combine the frozen mango, oat milk, protein powder, chia seeds, vanilla extract, and water.
  3. Blend everything on high for 30–60 seconds or until the mixture is completely smooth and creamy.
