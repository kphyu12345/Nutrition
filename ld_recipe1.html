Burger in a Bowl

Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 360 calories
  • 5.5g fiber
  • 26g protein

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef with garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper for 5–7 minutes, then drain.
  2. Mix sour cream, ketchup, soy sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, and maple syrup for the sauce.
  3. In a bowl, layer lettuce, beef, avocado, red onion, pickles, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Drizzle with sauce and season to taste.
