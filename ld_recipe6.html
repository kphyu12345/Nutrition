Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 286 calories , 25.5g protein , 2.9g fiber

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl combine all of the Kung Pao Sauce ingredients and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet over high heat, add in the chiles and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add in the chicken and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add in the bell peppers, celery, and onion. Lower the heat to medium-high heat and cook for 5 minutes, stirring often.
  5. Add in the garlic, ginger, and peanuts. Mix together and cook for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant. Add in the Kung Pao Sauce and cook for 1-2 minutes until the chicken is cooked through with no pink in the center, and the sauce is thick.
  6. Serve over rice
