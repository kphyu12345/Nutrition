Giant Oat Cookie

Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 69calories, 2.3g protein.

Instructions

  1. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, baking powder, pinch of salt).
  2. Make a well in the centre. Add the egg, milk and vanilla then whisk until combined and almost lump free.
  3. Melt just 1 teaspoon of butter in a non stick pan over medium high heat (or medium ,if your stove is strong).
  4. Pour about 1 1/2 tbsp batter into the pan and coax it into a circle.
  5. Cook for 1 1/2 minutes until bubbles rise to the surface
  6. Flip with a flick of the wrist, with confidence! Then cook the other side for 1 minute or until golden, then remove from the pan.
